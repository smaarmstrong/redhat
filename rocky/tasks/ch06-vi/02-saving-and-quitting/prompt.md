Read the exit instructions in section 6.1 and the "File operations" list in
6.6.5 (pp. 84 and 95): quitting with :q, saving with :w, saving and quitting
with :wq / :x / ZZ, forcing with !, and saving under another name.

the leading colon (matching ignores case and extra spaces). Concept questions
